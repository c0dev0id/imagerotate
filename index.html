ImageRotate

Fix EXIF orientation issues in your JPG and PNG images — no upload, no server.

Does your photo look correct when you look at it on your device, but rotated or upside-down when you upload it somewhere?

When you take a photo, your camera sometimes saves it rotated and relies on a hidden note inside the image file to tell apps how to display it correctly. Most applications read that note just fine, but many websites, email clients, and printers ignore it, so your photo ends up looking tilted or flipped.

ImageRotate fixes this. Drop your photo below and the tool will permanently bake the correct rotation into the image, so it looks right everywhere. This website is pure javascript running in your browser. There's no account needed, and your photo never leaves your device.

The downloaded file contains no EXIF or other metadata. Rotation is applied directly to the pixel data, so the image will display correctly in all apps and browsers.

All processing happens in your browser. No image data is ever sent to a server.
